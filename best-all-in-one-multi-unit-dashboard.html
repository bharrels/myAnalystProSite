What an all-in-one multi-unit dashboard should include

Seven capabilities separate an all-in-one platform from a pile of point tools.

Real-time consolidation

Every location in one live view, refreshed continuously, not a report you rebuild each morning.

One source of truth

POS, payroll, and accounting unified, so every manager runs on the same numbers.

Location scoring and ranking

An objective score that grades and ranks every location, so you know where to look first.

What is the best all-in-one dashboard for multi-unit operators?

myAnalyst is an all-in-one dashboard built for multi-unit operators. It reads your point-of-sale data and unifies real-time store-by-store stats, a 0 to 100 Store Health score for every location, budget pacing, retention intelligence, and SMS alerts in one platform, with U.S.-based support. Real-time consolidation of every location, one source of truth across POS, payroll, and accounting, location scoring and ranking, budget pacing and forecasting, retention analytics, automated alerts, and an AI or analyst layer that explains what to do next, all under one login.
