Retention · FAQ

Common questions about retention

What are the service lifecycle stages?

Every vehicle or household is placed in one of four stages based on its service cadence: on-track, due-soon, overdue, or lapsed. The mix shows at a glance how much of a store's base is healthy versus slipping away.

What is RFM segmentation?

RFM groups households by how Recently they visited, how Frequently, and how much Monetary value they bring. myAnalyst rolls that into named segments, Champions, Loyal, At-risk, and Hibernating, so outreach can be tailored to each.

How is at-risk revenue calculated?

At-risk revenue takes the households that are overdue or lapsing and multiplies them by their expected spend, so the cost of doing nothing is a dollar figure, not a vague worry.

Can I export due-for-service lists?

Yes. Every store can export its due-for-service and win-back lists, ranked by household CLV, ready to hand to the team or push into your marketing tool.
