The numbers you run on

Every auto-service metric, mapped to what myAnalyst shows

The signature metrics for quick-lube, tire, and repair, surfaced store by store with year-over-year deltas.

ARO (Average Repair Order)

Average revenue per ticket per shop, trended and ranked across the chain so you see where order value is climbing or slipping.

Car count

Cars per shop, per day, in LiveStats with YoY deltas color-coded so volume swings jump out.

Average ticket

Net sales over cars, side by side across stores, a fast read on whether your floor is upselling.

Labor %

Labor hours and dollars against sales, by shop. Payroll pulls in alongside POS, so cost sits right next to revenue.

Revenue per hour

Sales productivity by the hour in LiveStats, see how efficiently each shop turns open-bay time into revenue.

Repeat rate

How many guests come back, tracked at VIN and household level in Retention. The leading indicator of whether a shop is keeping its base.

Bay productivity

How hard each bay is working, shop by shop. Spot locations leaving capacity on the table during peak dayparts.

Technician efficiency

Throughput per technician across the chain, so coaching and staffing run on the same numbers everywhere.

Coupon ROI

What promotions return. Measure redemptions and the revenue behind them instead of guessing whether the offer paid off.

Questions

Auto-service analytics, answered

Does myAnalyst work with my existing point-of-sale system?

Yes. myAnalyst connects read-only to the systems you already run, POS, payroll, and QuickBooks. It does not replace your POS or accounting software; it reads from them and refreshes in near real-time. Most clients are live in days, not quarters.

Which auto-service metrics does myAnalyst track?

myAnalyst surfaces the numbers quick-lube, tire, and repair operators run on, ARO (Average Repair Order), car count, average ticket, labor %, revenue per hour, repeat rate, bay productivity, technician efficiency, and coupon ROI, store by store with year-over-year deltas. See the glossary for definitions.

Can I compare performance across all of my shops?

Yes. LiveStats shows every store in one grid and you can group by Market, Regional Manager, or District Manager. Store Health gives each location a 0 to 100 score and an A to F grade ranked against the rest of your chain, so you can see which shops need attention first.

How fast does the data update?

Data refreshes in near real-time across all your locations. Signals can also text or MMS a manager the moment a metric crosses a threshold you set, no app to open.
