Who we are

A software company based in Myrtle Beach, SC

SiteTech Systems builds and runs myAnalyst, a real-time analytics platform for chains that operate across many locations. It reads your point-of-sale data and turns it into live store-by-store dashboards, a 0 to 100 Store Health score for every store, budget pacing, retention intelligence, and SMS alerts. The data refreshes every five minutes, so the picture you see is the picture as it stands right now, not a report from last week.

Our approach

How we think about the work

We build the tool we wished we had when we were staring at forty stores' worth of exports every morning.

Operator-built

myAnalyst is shaped by how multi-unit operators actually run, the org runs VP to Regional Manager to District Manager to Store, and the product surfaces the right view for each seat instead of a generic chart library.

Read-only by design

We connect to the systems you already use, POS, payroll, QuickBooks, with a read-only connection. myAnalyst is not a POS or accounting replacement; it sits alongside your stack and reads from it.

Live in days, not quarters

Because we read your existing data rather than asking you to migrate it, most clients are live in days. No long rip-and-replace project, no waiting a quarter to see your own numbers.

100% U.S.-based support

When you call, you reach our team in Myrtle Beach, SC. Support is entirely U.S.-based, the same people who know the platform and your setup.
