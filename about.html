Who we are

A software company based in Myrtle Beach, SC

SiteTech Systems builds and runs myAnalyst for multi-location chains. It reads your POS data into live store-by-store dashboards, a 0 to 100 Store Health score per store, budget pacing, retention intelligence, and SMS alerts. Data refreshes in near real-time, so you see right now, not last week.

Our approach

How we think about the work

We built the tool we wished we had when we were staring at forty stores' worth of exports every morning.

Operator-built

Shaped by how multi-unit orgs run, VP to Regional to District to Store. Each seat gets the right view, not a generic chart library.

Read-only by design

We connect read-only to the systems you already use: POS, payroll, QuickBooks. myAnalyst is not a POS or accounting replacement; it reads alongside your stack.

Live in days, not quarters

We read your existing data instead of migrating it, so most clients are live in days. No rip-and-replace, no waiting a quarter to see your own numbers.

100% U.S.-based support

Call us and you reach our Myrtle Beach, SC team: the same people who know the platform and your setup.
