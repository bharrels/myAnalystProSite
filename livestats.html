LiveStats · FAQ

Common questions about LiveStats

How often does LiveStats refresh?

LiveStats refreshes every five minutes from your POS, so net sales, cars, labor, and ticket average are always current. There are no manual report runs and no overnight batch to wait on.

What metrics does LiveStats show?

Net sales, cars, full service, labor hours and dollars, ticket average, and revenue per hour, for every location, with color-coded this-year-vs-last-year deltas.

Can I group stores by Market, RM, or DM?

Yes. Group LiveStats by Market, Regional Manager, or District Manager and the outliers, both leaders and laggards, surface on their own so you know where to look first.

Can I drill into a single store?

Tap any store to drill in: that location's cars, ticket average, labor, vs-last-year delta, peak hour, and net sales by hour open inline, with no navigation away from the board.
