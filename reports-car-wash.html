FAQ

Car wash reporting questions

Can myAnalyst track unlimited-plan members separately from retail washes?

Yes. myAnalyst reads your POS read-only and separates recurring unlimited-plan members from single-wash retail traffic, so you can see membership mix (about 63% of washes in a typical club-heavy site), net member adds, churn, and the revenue each stream contributes per site and across the chain.

Does the chemical cost per car update in real time?

myAnalyst refreshes every 5 minutes, so wash counts, revenue per car, and capture rate update live throughout the day. Chemical cost per car is derived against your wash count, so it moves with volume and lets you spot a site whose variable cost per car drifts above the chain benchmark.

How does myAnalyst score a car wash location?

Every site gets a 0 to 100 Store Health score and an A to F grade built from five weighted pillars, Growth (25%), Goal (25%), Loyalty (20%), Standing (15%), and Operations (15%). For car wash, Loyalty leans on membership retention and Operations reflects capture rate and throughput, so the score reflects how a club-model wash actually performs. See how scoring works.

Can I build a custom car wash report?

Yes. The Report Builder lets you assemble drag-and-drop dashboards and saved trackers, for example a membership-churn-by-site view or a chemical-cost-per-car tracker, from any of the LiveStats, membership, financial, auditing, and geospatial data myAnalyst already reads from your POS.
