Operations

Volume, throughput & capture

The numbers your site managers and operations team live in, how many cars went through, how fast, how many you converted, and what each one was worth.

Wash Count & Throughput by Site

Live wash count for every site and the chain, about 9,420 washes on a strong portfolio day, with year-over-year deltas, hour-of-day and day-of-week throughput, and peak-hour cars per hour so you can match staffing and queue length to demand.

Capture Rate & Conversion

Capture rate by site, the share of arriving traffic that buys a wash, and the share of single washes that convert to an unlimited plan. Trends the upsell-to-membership conversion against the chain benchmark so you see which sites turn one-time guests into recurring revenue.

Revenue per Car & Wash Mix

Revenue per car (about $14.20 portfolio-wide) broken down by wash package, add-on attach rate (ceramic, tire shine, undercarriage), and the retail-vs-member split, so you can see whether a site's average is rising on mix and add-ons or just on price.

Unlimited-Plan Mix & Base

Active member count, plan-tier breakdown (basic / premium / ceramic), and membership mix as a share of total washes (about 63%), by site and chain-wide, with month-over-month base growth.

Membership Churn & Reactivation

Monthly and rolling churn rate per site, cancellation reasons where captured, win-back/reactivation rate, and the lift in net adds, so you can tell a healthy base from one quietly bleeding plans.

RFM Segments & At-Risk Members

Recency-frequency-monetary scoring on every member and household, due-soon and at-risk lists, customer lifetime value, and the dollar value of plans about to lapse, exportable for win-back outreach.

Finance

Where the money comes in, and goes out per car

Tie revenue streams, recurring billing, and the biggest variable cost in a wash, chemical, back to volume, by site.

Means of Payment & Recurring Billing

Revenue by means of payment, card, cash, app/wallet, and recurring membership billing, with the recurring-billing share, failed-payment rate, and the split between retail-wash and member revenue per site.

Chemical Cost per Car & Variable Cost

Chemical cost per car derived against wash count, trended per site against the chain benchmark, so a site over-dosing or running a mis-calibrated dosing system stands out, alongside other variable cost per car.

Paid In / Paid Out & Cash Reconciliation

Every paid-in and paid-out at the register, credit memos, and end-of-day cash reconciliation, with drawer over/short rolled up by site and shift so finance can close the books without chasing managers.

Loss Prevention & Auditing

Catch the leak before it compounds

Voids, discounts, and drawer variances are small per transaction and large across a chain. myAnalyst's auditing surfaces the outliers automatically.

Voids, Refunds & Aborted Sales

Void count and dollars, refunds, and aborted/re-rung sales ranked by site, register, and employee, with rate-per-wash so a site doing higher volume isn't flagged just for being busy.

Over / Short & Drawer Variance

Cash over/short by drawer, shift, and employee, with open/close variances and recurring-offender ranking, so a pattern at one site or one cashier surfaces instead of hiding in the daily noise.

Discount, Comp & Free-Wash Auditing

Discounts, comps, employee/free washes, and wildcard codes by reason and by employee, with data-capture quality scoring, flagging the sites where give-aways quietly erode revenue per car.

FAQ

Car wash reporting questions

Can myAnalyst track unlimited-plan members separately from retail washes?

Yes. myAnalyst reads your POS read-only and separates recurring unlimited-plan members from single-wash retail traffic, so you can see membership mix (about 63% of washes in a typical club-heavy site), net member adds, churn, and the revenue each stream contributes per site and across the chain.

Does the chemical cost per car update in real time?

myAnalyst refreshes every 5 minutes, so wash counts, revenue per car, and capture rate update live throughout the day. Chemical cost per car is derived against your wash count, so it moves with volume and lets you spot a site whose variable cost per car drifts above the chain benchmark.

How does myAnalyst score a car wash location?

Every site gets a 0 to 100 Store Health score and an A to F grade built from five weighted pillars, Growth (25%), Goal (25%), Loyalty (20%), Standing (15%), and Operations (15%). For car wash, Loyalty leans on membership retention and Operations reflects capture rate and throughput, so the score reflects how a club-model wash actually performs. See how scoring works.

Can I build a custom car wash report?

Yes. The Report Builder lets you assemble drag-and-drop dashboards and saved trackers, for example a membership-churn-by-site view or a chemical-cost-per-car tracker, from any of the LiveStats, membership, financial, auditing, and geospatial data myAnalyst already reads from your POS.
