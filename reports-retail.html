Margin & Finance

Where the money actually comes from

Fuel runs on pennies, inside runs on mix. The Financials module separates the two, exposes category margin, and reconciles the till.

Fuel Margin & Volume

Category & Department Margin

Means of Payment

Tender mix by store, cash, credit, debit, EBT, mobile, fleet cards, with card-fee drag on margin. Track the shift away from cash and reconcile each tender against the deposit.

Loss Prevention & Auditing

Find the leakage before month-end

On thin margins, a few percent of shrink or a quiet void habit matters. The Auditing & Compliance module flags it by store, register, cashier, and shift.

Shrink & Inventory Variance

Shrink as a percent of sales and in dollars by store and category, with variance trended over time so a creeping problem shows before the physical count confirms it.

Voids, Refunds & No-Sales

Void, refund, and no-sale counts and dollars by cashier and shift, with rate vs the chain norm so an outlier clerk surfaces instead of hiding in the total.

Over / Short & Cash Reconciliation

Over/short by drawer, cashier, and shift against expected cash. Flag the registers and operators that consistently miss, and reconcile each shift's deposit.

Discounts & Promotions Audit

Manual discounts, employee discounts, and promo redemptions by cashier and shift. See margin given away at the register and tell real promotions from discount abuse.

FAQ

Retail & c-store reporting questions

Can myAnalyst separate inside sales from fuel?

Yes. myAnalyst reads your POS read-only and splits merchandise (inside) sales from fuel. Track inside sales (around $58.4K per store), basket size (around $22.10), and inside margin (around 31.8%) on their own, while fuel volume and fuel margin in cents per gallon get their own report. View either stream by store, by shift, or across the whole chain.

How does myAnalyst help with c-store shrink and cash control?

The Auditing & Compliance and Financials modules surface shrink and inventory variance, voids, refunds, no-sales, over/short, discounts, and paid in / paid out by store, register, cashier, and shift. Signals texts a district manager the moment a register runs over/short past your threshold or a cashier's void rate spikes, so leakage is caught the same day, not at month-end.

How often do the retail dashboards refresh?

In near real-time. LiveStats shows inside sales, basket size, daypart mix, and margin for every location in near real time, with this-year-vs-last-year deltas color-coded so the laggard store never hides in the chain average.

Can I build my own c-store report if I don't see it here?

Yes. The Report Builder lets you drag and drop any metric myAnalyst reads, inside sales, fuel margin, basket size, category margin, voids, over/short, daypart, into a saved dashboard or tracker scoped to a store, district, or the whole portfolio. myAnalyst Pro then writes an AI monthly briefing in plain English on top of those numbers.
