FAQ

Multi-location dashboard questions

What operators ask most. More in the full FAQ and glossary.

How many locations can myAnalyst dashboards handle?

It serves 1,100+ locations across its client base and analyzes more than $2B in annual revenue. LiveStats rolls up by Market, Regional Manager, or District Manager, so the same dashboard works whether you run a handful of stores or hundreds.

Do I have to replace my POS or accounting system?

No. myAnalyst makes a read-only connection to your existing POS, payroll, and QuickBooks. It never replaces your POS or accounting software. Most clients are live in days, not quarters.

How often does the data refresh?

Every 5 minutes, so the store-by-store picture in LiveStats reflects today, not yesterday's exported report.

Can it work for industries other than auto service?

Yes. myAnalyst supports auto care, car wash, hospitality, QSR / fast food, fitness, retail / c-store, and home services, with signature metrics tuned to each. The dashboard, Store Health scoring, and budget pacing work for any multi-location operation.

How do my managers get alerted when a number moves?

The Signals add-on sends SMS and MMS alerts on custom rules by metric, location, and recipient, with escalation routing and daily summaries. A District Manager is texted the moment a store falls behind budget pace, with no app to open.
