How to read this page

Organized by the team that uses it

myAnalyst reads your POS and property systems read-only, refreshes every 5 minutes, and scores every property 0 to 100. Reports are grouped by use case: Operations, Finance, Property Health & Pacing, Loss Prevention & Auditing, Signals, and multi-property consolidation. New to a term? See the glossary.

Operations

RevPAR, occupancy, ADR & labor

The four numbers that decide whether a property had a good night, per property and rolled up to the portfolio, with year-over-year context.

RevPAR & Room Revenue report

Revenue per available room per property, day by day. Portfolio RevPAR (~$112 in the sample), room-revenue totals, and color-coded YoY deltas.

Occupancy & Availability report

Occupied vs available rooms by property and night (~81% portfolio occupancy), with sell-out flags, open-room counts, and pace to target.

Department Revenue report

Revenue split across rooms, food & beverage, spa, and other outlets per property, with total revenue, RevPAR contribution, and YoY change for each.

Daily Flash report

One-screen morning recap per property: RevPAR, occupancy, ADR, labor %, and revenue vs budget and last year. Ready before the stand-up.

Hospitality reporting FAQ

Questions operators ask

Can myAnalyst calculate RevPAR, occupancy, and ADR across all of my properties?

Yes. myAnalyst reads your POS and property data, computes RevPAR, occupancy, and ADR for every property, and consolidates them into one portfolio view with year-over-year deltas. In the sample data, RevPAR runs about $112 on roughly 81% occupancy. Numbers refresh every 5 minutes, so you never work from yesterday's export.

How does Store Health work for a hotel or resort portfolio?

Each property gets a single 0 to 100 Store Health score and an A to F grade from five weighted pillars: Growth 25%, Goal 25%, Loyalty 20%, Standing 15%, and Operations 15%, ranked against the rest of your portfolio. For hospitality, the pillars are fed by RevPAR and revenue growth, budget pace, repeat-guest loyalty, percentile standing versus peers, and operational metrics like labor %.

Will Signals text me when a property falls behind on revenue or labor?

Yes. Signals is an add-on that sends SMS and MMS alerts the moment a KPI crosses a threshold you set: a property pacing behind budget, occupancy below target, labor % over its ceiling, or an unusual void or over/short. Alerts reach the right general manager or regional director without anyone opening an app.

Can I build custom hospitality reports beyond the standard ones?

Yes. The drag-and-drop Report Builder assembles custom dashboards and saved trackers from any metric myAnalyst captures. Combine RevPAR, ADR, labor %, means of payment, and budget pace into the exact view your operations and finance teams need, then schedule it or pin it to a property scorecard.
